All About Oreo

Oreo is our bearded dragon. We have had her since January 2018. Honestly, I did not want to get her at the time, but my son talked us into it. Now, I am glad that we did. She has become a member of our family and I love her!

It is amazing to see show much she has grown from when we first brought her home.



What Oreo Wants Everyone To Know About Her

  • She spends most of her time in her terrarium.
  • She like to eat superworms and sometimes crickets.
  • She likes to eat blueberries too!
  • Most of the vegetables that she is supposed to eat, go in the trash.
  • Oreo runs very fast when she wants to.
  • She likes to be pet.
  • She likes to take a warm bath in the sun.
  • She watches television or videos on computers or tablets.
